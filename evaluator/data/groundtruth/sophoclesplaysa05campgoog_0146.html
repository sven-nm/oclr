The ἐκκύκλημα is now pushed on through the central door. It was a small stage, but large enough to allow of Ajax being shown surrounded by some of the slaugh- tered animals. The word πράγη in 347, and the language of vv. 351 ff., show that the carnage was represented. The other plays in which Sophocles has used the eccyclema are the Antigone (1294, where see n.), and the Electra (1464 f., n.).
